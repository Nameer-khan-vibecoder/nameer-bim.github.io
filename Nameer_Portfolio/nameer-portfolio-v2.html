About Me

From coordination model to factory floor

I am a BIM Engineer with over two years of UK-based MEP project experience, most recently at Merit — a leading offsite MEP manufacturer based in Newcastle upon Tyne. My work has centred on complex healthcare environments, contributing to the full coordination and fabrication workflow.

My background covers Revit MEP modelling, clash detection in Navisworks, and the production of fabrication-ready 2D deliverables — including PAM drawings, spool drawings, HVAC coordination views, and setting-out documentation issued directly for factory use.

I hold an MSc in Engineering Management, which complements my technical skills with a strong foundation in project planning, coordination leadership, and programme delivery. I am actively seeking opportunities in MEP BIM Coordination, VDC, and Project Management roles across the GCC, UK, Europe, and Australia.
